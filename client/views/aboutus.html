About the Team

Pic-A-farm is a concept application built for the Greenfield project for Hack Reactor. The project is based off our mutual love for organic vegetables. Our app allows home growers to connect to consumers that enjoy receiving organic vegetables at home. Consumers that participate in a particular farm is subscribed to updates and after harvest will receive a box of vegetables that they requested.
